How to Reach Us

By Car

Take the main road towards Nandhi hills, turn right at Road no 8, and look for the school near anjaneya swamy temple.

By Bus

Take any bus route that goes to Meerpet and get down at Nandhi hills stop. The school is a 5-minute walk from there.

By Train

The nearest railway station is Hyderabad Deccan Station. From there, take a bus or taxi to reach the school.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

Admissions are open for the 2025-26 academic year. Please contact us for specific requirements based on the class level you're interested in.

What are the school timings?

School hours are from 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM, Monday to Saturday.

Do you provide transport facilities?

Yes, we provide safe and reliable transport service for students. Please contact us for route details and pricing.

What curriculum do you follow?

We follow CBSE & State Syllabus with Activity Based Method, offering education from Nursery to 10th Class in English Medium.

Do you have sports facilities?

Yes, we have a big playground and offer various sports including cricket, basketball, table tennis, and athletics.
